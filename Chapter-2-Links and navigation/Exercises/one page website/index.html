Oppenheimer

Overview of the plot

Against the shadowy background of World War II, the drama traces Oppenheimer's appointment by General Leslie Groves to head the Manhattan Project. Scientists work under his leadership to create the atomic bomb, the climax of which is the Trinity test—the first nuclear explosion—and changing the face of history forever. The drama also traces Oppenheimer's post-war life, including political interrogation and intra-personal conflict.

Review of the movie

Time magazine praised the film, citing, "Christopher Nolan's 'Oppenheimer,' with Cillian Murphy, is praised for sweeping scale and gritty effort to tell life of J. Robert Oppenheimer."It won seven Academy Awards at the 96th Academy Awards, with the Best Picture, Best Director going to Nolan, Best Actor to Murphy, and Best Supporting Actor to Downey Jr.All in all, "Oppenheimer" is an immersion into one of the greatest minds of our era, adding A-grade performances to Nolan's script to present a cerebral experience on screen
