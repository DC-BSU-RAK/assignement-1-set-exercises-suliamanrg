Choco Cookies

Description

Choco cookies are delicious, crunchy or soft cookies filled with rich, melted chocolate or chocolate chips. They are perfectly balanced in sweetness and cocoa flavor to be the favorite treat of young and old. Excellent for snacking or sharing with friends and family!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup sugar(100 g)
  • ¾ cup brown sugar(150 g)
  • ½ cup unsalted butter(100 g)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour(150 g)
  • ½ teaspoon baking sod
  • 4 oz milk or sweet chocolate chunks(110 g)
  • 4 oz dark chocolate chunk(100 g)

Preparation

  • Whisk the butter, salt, and sugars together in a large bowl until they are a smooth paste with no lumps.
  • Whisk the egg and vanilla together and beat until light ribbons are formed that fall from the whisk and hold for a moment before falling back into the mixture.
  • Add the flour and baking soda in and fold with a spatula (Don't overmix, which would toughen the gluten in the flour resulting in cookies that are more caky).
  • Fold in the chocolate pieces, and then refrigerate the dough for a minimum of 30 minutes. To concentrate the toffee flavor and color, refrigerate overnight. The longer the dough rests, the more complex its flavor will be.
  • Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Use an ice cream scoop to portion out the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, at least 4 inches (10 cm) apart and 2 inches (5 cm) away from pan edges so the cookies can spread.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until edges have just started to barely brown.
  • Allow to cool completely before serving.
  • Enjoy!
